Travel

I have a keen interest in traveling. I have visited many places in the past and look forward to travelling more in the future. As you can see from my pictures below i recently visited South Africa, Which was amazing we visited so many amazing places Such as Robben Island , which was a place of isolation, where political prisoners, including Nelson Mandela, were imprisoned and cut off from the rest of the world. It also served as a voluntary sanctuary for lepers. This was extreamly interesting and provided an insight into the countrys history. We also visited Aquila Private Game Reservese here we got to see wild game in their natural environment from the vantage point of a 4x4 offroad vehicle, this allowed us to get as close as we dared to Elephants, Lions and buffalos. Finally we took a cable cart up Table moutain and took in the spectular views of the city from the "lions head". These were some of my favourite memories from South Africa and it makes me excited to travel more in the future
